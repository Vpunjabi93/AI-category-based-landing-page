## 6. OBJECTIONS / FAQ

**Section header:** Common Questions Before You Apply

**Is this a degree or a certificate?**
It's a certificate, not a degree — issued by the institute's continuing/executive education body (CEP at IIT Delhi, CEC at IIT Roorkee, CTEL at DTU, or IIM Nagpur directly for the managers' programme). It carries the institute's name and academic rigour, but it won't appear as a degree on your transcript.

**I work long hours — can I keep up?**
All programmes run on a weekend format, spread across 6-8 months specifically so working professionals can manage the weekly load alongside a full-time job. [Recorded session availability varies by programme — confirm per programme before stating as a blanket guarantee.]

**Won't the GenAI curriculum be outdated by the time I finish?**
Programmes are revised between batches to reflect current techniques — for example, the IIT Delhi GenAI curriculum already covers current methods like RAG, fine-tuning strategies and responsible AI safeguards. [EXTRAPOLATED framing — validate "revised between batches" claim with programme teams before publishing; only the IIT Delhi GenAI module list is brochure-sourced, not a general revision policy.]

**Do I need coding experience?**
It depends on the programme. Several (IIT Delhi AI&ML for Industry, IITM Pravartak) are open to freshers and don't assume prior coding background. IIM Nagpur's programme for managers requires no coding at all. Others (Generative AI, AI/ML/DL) expect some existing technical or maths foundation. Use the filter above to find programmes that match your starting point.

**Is the fee negotiable, and are there payment options?**
Each programme has its own instalment schedule, generally split across 3-4 payments after an initial application fee. EMI and loan options are available on select programmes — these are services offered by TimesPro, not the partner institute, so terms should be confirmed directly with a counsellor.
