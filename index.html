Common Questions Before You Apply

It's a certificate, not a degree — issued by the institute's continuing/executive education body (CEP at IIT Delhi, CEC at IIT Roorkee, CTEL at DTU, or IIM Nagpur directly for the managers' programme). It carries the institute's name and academic rigour, but it won't appear as a degree on your transcript.

All programmes run on a weekend format, spread across 6-8 months specifically so working professionals can manage the weekly load alongside a full-time job.

It depends on the programme. Several (IIT Delhi's AI & ML for Industry, IIT Madras Pravartak) are open to freshers and don't assume prior coding background. IIM Nagpur's programme for managers requires no coding at all. Others (Generative AI, AI/ML/DL) expect some existing technical or maths foundation. Use the filter above to find programmes that match your starting point.

Each programme has its own instalment schedule, generally split across 3-4 payments after an initial application fee. EMI and loan options are available on select programmes — these are services offered by TimesPro, not the partner institute, so terms should be confirmed directly with a counsellor.
